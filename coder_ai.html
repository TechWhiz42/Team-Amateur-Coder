About Us

Welcome to the Programming And Tech Skills Club! Our vision is to foster a vibrant community of aspiring programmers, empowering them to explore the exciting world of technology and build innovative solutions. Our mission is to provide a platform for learning, collaboration, and growth through workshops, projects, and events.

Founded

Established in 2020, the club started with a small group of students passionate about coding.

First Workshop

Conducted our first workshop on web development in 2021.

Community Growth

Expanded our membership and started organizing regular events by 2023.
